Dungeons / Trials / Raids

A few quick tips to help you when you load into a dungeon, trial, or raid.

Everyone

Stand in the Good Puddles!

These will heal you and/or reduce damage taken!

White Mage Scholar Astrologian
Heals those in it Heals / mitigates damage Heals / Damage

These do damage to enemies when they stand in it!

Ninja Dark Knight Summoner
Does damage to targets in it Does damage to targets in it Does damage to targets in it

If you die in battle

  • …and are raised: DO NOT: CAST, PERFORM AN ACTION, PERFORM A WEAPONSKILL. You have a few seconds of invulnerability. Give the healer a chance to heal you back up.
  • …you will get a weakness debuff. This means your main stat (STR, DEX, INT, MIND) is reduced by 25%.
  • If you die again while under the weakness effect, you get the Brink of Death debuff which reduces those stats down by 50%.

Tanks

Put on Emnity (threat) Stance

DRK: Grit WAR: Defiance GNB: Royal Guard PLD: Iron Will

Minimize Movement

Melee jobs have positionals and you make them lose damage by moving a lot.

Provoke is not used on cooldown

All provoke does is set your emnity to be equal to the highest level eminity currently on that target and then gives you a tiny bit extra emnity on top. Even if you are the one on top! Save it for an emergancy.

Pace your use of mitigations, do not use them all at once

  • ROLE BASED: Rampart, Reprisal, Arm’s Length
  • PLD: Sheltron, Sentinal, and others
  • WAR: Thill of Battle, Vengeance, Raw Intuition, Equilibrium, and others
  • GNB: Camouflage, Nebula, Aurora (good for pulling with heal emnity), and others
  • DRK: Shadow Wall, Dark Mind, Dark Missionary, and others

Your Immunities should not be used on cooldown, save for dire purposes (large aoe pack pulls as an example)

  • PLD: Hallowed Ground
  • WAR: Holmgang
  • GNB: Superbolide
  • DRK: Living Dead

Healers

  • Check your party’s gear, you’ll want to know if they are undergeared for the level dungeon/trial/raid you are doing.
  • Be sure you DPS, future content requires healers to DPS and heal. Get used to it now.

DPS

Don’t stand in front of the boss

It’s a good way to get cleaved or tankbusted and die. Dead DPS do no dps.

If you’re ranged, stay near healer

Healers have heals that are mostly centered around themselves (there are exceptions), but this helps them to be able to do top ups quickly from raidwides.

If you decide to stay as far from everyone as possible, do not be surprised if a healer lets you die*
